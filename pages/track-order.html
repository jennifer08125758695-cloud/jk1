Helpful Tracking Tips

1
Tracking information is emailed to you when your order ships. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for a message with the subject "Your VOGUE USA order has shipped."
2
It can take up to 24 hours for your tracking number to become active on the carrier's website after receiving your shipment confirmation email.
3
If your tracking shows "Delivered" but you haven't received your package, check with neighbors and your building front desk. Contact us if it's still missing after 48 hours.
4
If you need further assistance, contact our support team at support@vogueusamodels.com or call 1-800-864-8472.
