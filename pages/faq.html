Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about orders, shipping, returns, and your account.

Orders & Payment
Browse our collections, select your size and color, then click "Add to Cart." When you're ready, click the cart icon at the top right and proceed to checkout. You'll enter your shipping address and payment information to complete your order.
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are processed through a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway with 256-bit SSL encryption.
You have a 1-hour window to modify or cancel your order after placement. After that, your order enters processing and changes may not be possible. Please contact us immediately at orders@vogueusamodels.com or call 1-800-864-8472 if you need to make changes.
Yes! You'll receive an email confirmation within a few minutes of placing your order. This email will include your order number, item details, and estimated delivery date. Please check your spam folder if you don't see it.
Absolutely. Our website uses HTTPS with 256-bit SSL encryption for all data transmission. We never store your full credit card number on our servers. All payment processing is handled by PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processors.
Shipping & Delivery
Standard shipping (5–7 business days) is FREE on all orders over $75. For orders under $75, standard shipping is $9.99. Express shipping (2–3 business days) is $14.99. Overnight shipping is $29.99. All rates are for domestic USA delivery.
Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days. Express takes 2–3 business days. Overnight shipping delivers by the next business day if ordered before 2PM EST. Processing time (1–2 business days) is separate from shipping time.
Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with your tracking number. You can use this number at our tracking page, USPS.com, or UPS.com. Allow up to 24 hours for tracking information to appear.
Yes, we ship to Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, and select European countries. International shipping rates vary by destination and are calculated at checkout. Customers are responsible for any import duties or customs fees charged by their country.
Returns & Exchanges
We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with all tags attached. Undergarments and items marked "Final Sale" are not eligible for return. See our full Return Policy for details.
Email returns@vogueusamodels.com with your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a prepaid USPS return label within 24 business hours. Pack the item securely and drop it at any USPS location.
Refunds are processed within 5–10 business days after we receive and inspect your return. The refund is issued to your original payment method. Credit card refunds may take an additional 3–5 business days to appear on your statement.
We sincerely apologize for the error. Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with your order number and photos of the issue. We will send you a prepaid return label and ship the correct or replacement item at no charge, or issue a full refund.
Sizing & Fit
We recommend checking our Size Guide before purchasing. Each product page also includes specific sizing notes. When between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for the most comfortable fit. Our sizes follow standard US sizing.
Yes! We carry sizes XS through 3X in most women's styles and S through 2XL in men's styles. Plus-size options are clearly marked in the size selector on each product page. We are continuously expanding our size range to be more inclusive.
Account & Password
No, you can check out as a guest. However, creating a free account gives you benefits like saved addresses, order history, faster checkout, exclusive member-only discounts, and early access to sales.
Click "Login" at the top of the page, then click "Forgot password?" Enter your email address and we'll send you a reset link within a few minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact us at support@vogueusamodels.com.
