About VOGUE USA

The story of America's premier online fashion destination.

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

Our Story

VOGUE USA was founded in New York City in 2018 with one clear mission: to make premium American fashion accessible to everyone, everywhere. We believe that great style should not come with an impossible price tag — and we have built our entire business around that belief.

What started as a small curated boutique has grown into one of the most trusted online fashion destinations in the United States, proudly serving over two million customers across all 50 states.

What We Offer

We offer a carefully curated selection of clothing, shoes, and accessories for Men, Women, and Kids. From everyday essentials to special occasion pieces, every item in our collection is hand-selected by our team of experienced buyers to ensure quality, style, and value.

Our Values

Quality First

Every product is inspected before it reaches our warehouse. We partner only with manufacturers who meet our rigorous quality standards.

Accessibility

We believe fashion is for everyone. Our diverse size ranges — from XS to 3X — and our inclusive pricing reflect our commitment to serving all Americans.

Sustainability

We are actively working to reduce our environmental footprint. We use recycled packaging materials, partner with eco-conscious suppliers, and continually evaluate our supply chain for sustainability improvements.

Customer Obsession

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. Our support team is available six days a week, we offer free returns on most items, and we stand behind every product we sell with our 30-day satisfaction guarantee.

Our Team

Our diverse team of fashion experts, technologists, and customer service professionals is based across the United States. United by a passion for great fashion and exceptional service, we work every day to bring you the best online shopping experience possible.

Get In Touch

We would love to hear from you. Whether you have a question, a suggestion, or just want to share your style with us, reach out at hello@vogueusamodels.com or visit our Contact page.
